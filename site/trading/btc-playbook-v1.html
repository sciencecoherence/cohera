BTC Playbook v1 (Jalapablo-Adapted)

Risk-first framework. Educational only, not financial advice.

Core risk rules

  • Risk per trade: 1% max
  • Daily max loss: 2%
  • Weekly max loss: 5%

Setups

A) Absorb → Raid → Reclaim (Long)
Use at key support/liquidity-low with reclaim confirmation and stop below sweep low.

B) Distribution → Breakdown Retest (Short)
Use in weak context with failed retest and stop above rejection high.

Execution standards

  • HTF bias from Weekly/Daily; execute on 4H
  • Minimum target: 2R
  • No setup = no trade
